## Hotkeys:

Start/Stop: `space`

| | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| MIN neighbors to LIVE | `q` | `w` | `e` | `r` | `t` | `y` | `u` | `i` |
| MAX neighbors to LIVE | `a` | `s` | `d` | `f` | `g` | `h` | `j` | `k` |
| N neighbors to BE BORN | `z` | `x` | `c` | `v` | `b` | `n` | `m` | `,` |
